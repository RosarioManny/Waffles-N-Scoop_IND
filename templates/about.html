Our Story

Meet Mr. Harold "Harry" Thompson, an ice cream artisan whose life has been a delightful swirl of passion and perseverance. For six decades, Harry has been crafting ice cream, starting in his grandmother’s buisness. Using the hand-cranked machine and turning his love for the craft into a thriving business, Waffle N' Scoop. He’s poured his heart into every batch, creating flavors that range from classic strawberry to inventive delights like Coffee Javachip and Cotton Candy Blitz.

“Ice cream is my happiness”

He says, his hands dusted with powdered sugar. “When I see someone’s face light up after that first bite, it fills my heart. This isn’t just a business—it’s my life’s work.” Harry’s dedication and kindness have made him a local legend, spreading sweetness, one scoop at a time.
