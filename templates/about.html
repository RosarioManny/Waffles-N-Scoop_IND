Our Story

Meet Mr. Harold "Harry" Thompson, the 70-year-old ice cream artisan whose life has been a delightful swirl of passion and perseverance. Since his teenage years, Harry has been crafting ice cream, starting in his grandmother’s buisness. Using the hand-cranked machine and turning his love for the craft into a thriving business, Waffle N' Scoop. For over five decades, he’s poured his heart into every batch, creating flavors that range from classic vanilla bean to inventive delights like lavender honey and salted caramel pretzel. But for Harry, it’s not just about the ice cream—it’s about the joy it brings. “Ice cream is my happiness,” he says, his hands dusted with powdered sugar. “When I see someone’s face light up after that first bite, it fills my heart. This isn’t just a business—it’s my life’s work.” Harry’s dedication and kindness have made him a local legend, spreading sweetness, one scoop at a time.
